Rocket League Quiz

This quiz based only on RLCSX and onward and consists of 25 questions.

It's a two-part quiz. The first part consists of 15 questions where you'll be given 4 possible answers with only one being correct.

In the second part, which consists of 10 questions, you'll only be given a question and an input window, where you must type in a correct answer.

See for yourselves how well you know the "open-era" of Rocket League.

Good luck!
